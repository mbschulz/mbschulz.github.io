Free boundary minimal surfaces with the same symmetry group as one of the Platonic solids

Conjecturally, the Euclidean unit ball contains families of free boundary minimal surfaces with increasing genus and the same symmetry group as one of the Platonic solids. Some of the surfaces with genus zero could be included as first members of these families.
