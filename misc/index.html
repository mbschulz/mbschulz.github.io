Schwarz' D-Surface, Gyroid and Schwarz' P-Surface

Hermann Amandus Schwarz' P- and D-surface and Alan Schoen's Gyroid are all part of the same associate family of triply periodic minimal surfaces: They are locally isometric by means of a suitable Bonnet-transformation. The D-surface appears for a Bonnet angle of 0°, the Gyroid appears for a Bonnet angle of about 38.01477° and a Bonnet angle of 90° corresponds to the P-surface.
